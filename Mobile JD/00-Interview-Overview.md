## Interview Rounds (Expected)

1. **Prelim-HR** - Background, experience, expectations
2. **Written** - Technical assessment
3. **Technical-1** - Core mobile development
4. **Technical-2** - Architecture & problem-solving
5. **Management** - Culture fit, soft skills

> [!tip] Preparation Strategy
> Focus 70% on Android + iOS fundamentals, 20% on architecture/testing, 10% on soft skills.

## F2F Screen-Sharing Tips

> [!warning] Critical for Technical Rounds
> - Practice coding while explaining your thought process
> - Be ready to design a mobile app architecture on whiteboard/screen
> - Know how to debug common issues live
> - Review [[14-System-Design-Mobile]] and [[15-Live-Coding-Scenarios]]
